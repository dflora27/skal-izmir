Since 1934

Bringing Together Travel and Tourism Professionals Worldwide.

With its 8500-year history, unique nature, and endless energy, Izmir is one of the most vibrant stops where the Skål spirit lives.

A center of trade and culture from ancient times to the present, Izmir keeps hospitality alive in every corner, from Ephesus to Pergamon, from the narrow streets of Kemeraltı to the modern coast of Kordon.

As Skål Izmir, we blend this deep-rooted tourism potential of our city with our global friendship network, continuing to introduce the 'Pearl of the Aegean' to the world.

A Tourism Network Growing With Friendship

By bringing together the local professionals of the industry, Skål Izmir Club offers not just a business network, but a family environment shaping the future of Izmir tourism.

We contribute to our city's sustainable tourism goals and represent Izmir's values on global platforms together with our members.
