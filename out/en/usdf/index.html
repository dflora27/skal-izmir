USDF Skål Türkiye

Federation of International Skål Associations

We Have a Very Important Place in the World Skål Movement

International Skål entered Turkey with the establishment of the Istanbul Skål Club in 1956. SKAL TURKEY has been a defining force in the global tourism friendship network since then.

Federative Structure

Since March 2011, it has gained a federative structure and taken the name FEDERATION OF INTERNATIONAL SKAL ASSOCIATIONS (USDF).

Our Primary Goal

In addition to supporting growth, providing direction and support to the clubs it harbors, and generating projects.

Strengthening its weight in the sector by organizing useful meetings that provide added value, and raising awareness in public and the sector.

"To establish friendship, a common understanding, and cooperation among clubs and its members working in the Tourism Industry."
