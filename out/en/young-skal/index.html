Young Skål

A global organization established to maintain the dynamism of Skål, ensure its continuity, and raise successful tourism leaders of the future.

In Tourism Management
Young and Vibrant Blood

Young Skål members are young tourism professionals and students receiving tourism education.

"The first in the world to answer Skål's call to the youth and report it to Skål International's headquarters in Torremolinos by organizing the first Young Skål groups in 1999 were Turkish youths."

The main purpose is to ensure the personal and professional development of the youth, and in the following process, to make them well-equipped and mature tourism professionals.

Global Vision

Instant access to tourism networks at international standards.

Professional Development

Academic presentations, seminars, and direct executive mentorship.

Networking

Enormous career steps progressing from internship to General Management.

Leadership

Representing at the club level by managing projects and taking responsibilities.

The Founding Story in Turkey

The first Young Skål groups in the world were established in Antalya and Istanbul. The foundation of the story was laid in cooperation with Antalya Akdeniz University.

October 1998

Official Application

Application was made to the Associations Desk for the Antalya Young Skål Club. Bylaws corrections were carried out.

December 9, 1998

Official Establishment

With the approval of the Antalya Governorship, the Antalya Young Skål Association was officially founded. (16 members, 9 Board Members) Founding President: Özen Kırant.

March 19-21, 1999

Mediterranean Regional Meeting & Gala

With the participation of World Skål President Antonio Espinosa and Secretary General Jim Power, the Young Skål Antalya Foundation Ceremony and Gala Night were held at the Falez Hotel. The green 'Young Skål' badge was pinned on the youth by the World President.

May 23, 1999

First General Assembly

The First General Assembly of the Young Skål Association after its establishment was held. The founding 9 members were re-elected to the Board of Directors.

November 2-5, 1999

60th World Skål Congress Representation

Attendance was provided representing the Antalya Young Skål Club at the 60th World Skål Congress held in Stuttgart, Germany.

December 1999

University Promotions

Introductory meetings were organized for students at Akdeniz University Tourism Management Medicine and Social Sciences Vocational Schools, and many new visionary young people were added to the association.

1999-2000

Educational Seminars

Senior Hotel and Travel Agency managers and academicians participated as speakers in the monthly meetings organized.

2000 - 2001

National Marketing Congress & Symposium

At the Cultural Tourism Symposium (Falez Hotel) and the 5th National Marketing Congress, Young Skål members took active roles from preparation to the gala night, and the 1st Young Skål National Meeting was held.

As Izmir, we resolutely continue our vision of raising young leaders.
